Amazing things you can do with GHOST

THE PREDICTION

They make a free choice… But you already knew.

Before anyone says a word, you type something into a borrowed phone and set it face down on a table. You invite someone to type a freely chosen thought into their phone (it can be anything, but for maximum impact, provide them with a category, such as a celebrity, a country, etc.). Then, when your phone is turned over, it reveals a perfect match. Their thought. Already typed. Already known.

TELEKINESIS

They think it. Their phone types it.

They don't say it. They don't type it. They just think it. With their attention fixed on their phone, letters begin to appear—one by one—forming the exact thought they've kept entirely to themselves. As if their thoughts are being pulled straight into the search bar. No voice. No input.

FACE ID

The moment they open their eyes… everything changes.

They close their eyes and focus on a thought. A borrowed iPhone is raised toward their face. "Did you know Face ID only works when your eyes are open? Now imagine you could use your eyes to communicate more than just who you are, but also what you are thinking." You invite them to open their eyes, and suddenly the phone changes to showing images of what they are thinking.

THE GIFT

A memory from their childhood… drawn to life.

A spectator uses their own phone to search for gift they once adored as a child - it can be anything, but ideally something deeply personal or nostalgic. You ask them to set their phone aside or put it away, asking them to focus their thoughts on the gift and its memories. Then, to everyone's astonishment (and often yells of disbelief), you grab a pen and slowly draw the exact thing they are thinking.
